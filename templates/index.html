Combatting Employment Fraud

Job scams have become highly sophisticated, targeting fresh graduates and career changers. Fraudulent postings often seek to steal personal identities, gather bank credentials, or charge illegal upfront "fees".

Our platform employs Supervised Machine Learning Classifiers trained on thousands of real and fake job postings. By applying Natural Language Processing (NLP) techniques, the system extracts critical text features from job descriptions, salaries, benefits, and requirements to deliver a fast security assessment.
