How to Spot Job Scams

Scammers target active job hunters using fake roles. Look out for these signs before applying:

  • Upfront Training Fees
    Legitimate employers never charge candidates money to apply, join, buy laptops, or undergo "mandatory registration".
  • External Messaging Channels
    Interviews conducted exclusively on WhatsApp, Telegram, or Google Hangouts are major red flags.
  • Wire Transfers or Reshipping
    If the job description mentions receiving money in personal accounts, purchasing gift cards, or reforwarding packages, it is a money mule scam.
